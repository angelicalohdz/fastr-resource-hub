HMIS Data Quality Assessment Workflow

Systematic evaluation of facility-level monthly health data quality

INPUT: Monthly Facility Data

facility_id, period_id, indicator, count

Raw health facility reporting data aggregated at monthly level across all indicators

1

Data Loading & Preparation

  • Convert dates to standardized format
  • Detect and assign geographic hierarchy
  • Create composite indicators from component data
  • Validate facility identifiers and metadata
↓
2

Outlier Detection

  • Calculate median and median absolute deviation (MAD)
  • Flag statistical outliers using MAD multiplier (typically 2.5×)
  • Flag proportional outliers where a single value dominates
  • Generate outlier flags and facility-level alerts
↓
3

Completeness Assessment

  • Generate expected full time series for each facility-indicator pair
  • Identify missing monthly reports
  • Flag inactive facilities (zero reporting)
  • Calculate completeness percentages by facility and indicator
↓
4

Consistency Analysis

  • Aggregate facility-level data to district level
  • Calculate indicator ratios and relationships (e.g., deliveries vs. ANC1)
  • Apply evidence-based benchmarks for logical consistency
  • Assign consistency scores to individual facilities
↓
5

DQA Scoring

  • Filter to core indicators specified in DQA framework
  • Combine quality dimensions (completeness, consistency, accuracy)
  • Calculate facility-level composite DQA scores
  • Assign DQA categories (Good, Acceptable, Poor)

OUTPUTS

Outlier flags & facility lists
Completeness status report
Consistency results & ratios
DQA scores by facility
Quality dimension breakdowns
Trend analysis visualizations
